### Incorrect SMB Server Address or Share Name: Verify the `source` parameter in the `StorageClass` is correct. ### Insufficient Permissions: Ensure the service account and Azure credentials have the necessary permissions. ### Network Issues: Ensure that the Windows node can reach the SMB server and the necessary ports are open. ### Driver Version: Ensure you are using a compatible version of the CSI SMB driver with your Kubernetes cluster.
